Welcome to iCTERM!

Get to know the Integrated Laboratory for Cellular Tissue Engineering and Regenerative Medicine

Lung Tissue Engineering

Investigating the effects of hypoxia on differentiation of mouse embryonic stem cells (mESCs) and their subsequent differentiation into distal pulmonary epithelial cells.

Lung Tissue Engineering

Decellularizing rat lungs in order to deliver endothelial cells to create blood vessels within a bio-engineered lung.

Wound Healing

Electrospinning soy into 3D scaffolds to use as wound dressings in animal models of wound healing.

Bone Tissue Engineering

Electrospun chitosan-hydroxyapatite bio-composites in conjunction with bone progenitor cells might serve as bioactive tissue-engineered bone scaffolds

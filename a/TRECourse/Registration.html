Tissue and Regenerative Engineering Short Course

December 12-16, 2011

Philadelphia, PA

Registration Information

Fee Includes: All course materials, laboratory fees, all breakfasts and lunches and a dinner/happy hour each week.

Registration will open mid-October, 2011.

•$2,200 – Standard Fee

•$1,100– Academic Rate

The Tissue and Regenerative Engineering Short Course is being offered by the School of Biomedical Engineering, Science and Health Systems at Drexel University, in Philadelphia, PA. Classes will be taught by BIOMED faculty and the labs will be overseen by experienced BIOMED staff and senior graduate students.

Check-in will be at 7:30am of each Monday at the Bossone Research Enterprise Center (3120 Market Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104). Use the Bossone East elevators to reach the 7th floor, room 709 will be to your right. Classes and laboratories will be taught in Bossone and each day will begin in room 709.

Accommodations:

Discounted rates at some area hotels are available for Drexel visitors, but Drexel must be mentioned in order to get the discounted rate.

•The Sheraton University City hotel is within walking distance to our school.  The fee per night is $147.00.

•The Cornerstone Bed and Breakfast is within walking distance to our school and they offer $30.00 off regular price for any Drexel Guest.

•The Hotel Palomar in Center City is very nice and is close to shops, dining, etc. The fee per night is $147.00. 


Refund Policy: Full refund requests must be made in writing. Full refunds will be granted for cancellations received before November 25, 2011. Cancellations received between November 26-December 6, 2011 will be subject to a $300 cancellation fee. No refunds will be granted for cancellations after December 7, 2011.

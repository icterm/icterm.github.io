The goal of the HUB symposium is to bring together and pair up premier scientists and engineers from a number of Colleges at Drexel University and the Institute for Drug Research at the Hebrew University, Jerusalem in order to define and advance joint translational biomedical research projects, which will result in the creation and subsequent commercialization of new pharmaceuticals, enhanced drug delivery technologies, promising biomedical devices, and improved biomedical procedures.
